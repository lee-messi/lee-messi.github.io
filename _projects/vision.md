In this project, we investigate whether GPT-4, a multimodal LLM, reproduces racial stereotypes when asked to write about images depicting racial/ethnic groups. We focus on multiple facets of racial stereotyping including, and not limited to, the tendency to associate groups with specific traits, such as names or place of residence (e.g., urban v. rural) and to depict the socially subordinate racial/ethnic group (i.e. African Americans) as less diverse (i.e., homogeneity bias) than its socially dominant counterpart (i.e. White Americans).

For the time being, you can read the [pre-registration report](https://osf.io/epukv/) uploaded on OSF.
