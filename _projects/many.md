In this project, we build on top of the foundation laid out by [my previous work on homogeneity bias in LLMs](https://openreview.net/forum?id=izC60DLg29). Expanding beyond the initial scope of race/ethnicity and gender, this new phase delves into a more comprehensive exploration of how LLMs perceive a variety of social group identities, including age, political orientation, and socioeconomic status. This shift is crucial in deepening our understanding of bias within AI systems. By examining a broader spectrum of social identities, we aim to uncover nuanced ways in which LLMs interact with and represent these diverse groups. This research is pivotal for highlighting potential areas of improvement in AI development, contributing to the creation of more equitable AI technologies in the future.
