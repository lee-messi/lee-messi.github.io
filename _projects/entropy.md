In this project, we build on top of the foundation laid out by [my previous work on homogeneity bias in LLMs](https://openreview.net/forum?id=izC60DLg29). In this project, we expand beyond this work by 1) employing text completion prompts to examine jobs that LLMs associate with various gender and racial/ethnic groups, and by 2) proposing a novel measure of homogeneity in LLM-generated text - entropy. This measure quantifies homogeneity bias by assessing the uncertainty of LLM-generated text (i.e., jobs, industry) across groups.
