Greetings! I am currently a fourth-year PhD Candidate at Washington University in St. Louis, specializing in the Psychological & Brain Sciences track within the Division of Computational and Data Sciences. I serve as the primary graduate student at the [Diversity Science Lab](https://sites.wustl.edu/calvinlai/).

My research lies at the intersection of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Social Psychology. Specifically, I delve into the examination of biases inherent in Natural Language Processing (NLP) systems, with an emphasis on the representational biases surrounding racial and ethnic groups within Large Language Models (LLMs) like ChatGPT. Through my work, I uncover human-like biases reproduced by these systems and explore effective strategies to mitigate their societal impact.
